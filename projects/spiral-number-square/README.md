Spiral Number Square
====================

The code is meant to generate a spiral number square of size n.

The spiral number square is a square matrix with numbers increasing in a
spiral pattern.

Examples
--------

+ Size: 5
+ From center
+ Clockwise

```
21 22 23 24 25 
20 7  8  9  10 
19 6  1  2  11 
18 5  4  3  12 
17 16 15 14 13 
```

+ Size: 7
+ From center
+ Counterclockwise

```
49 48 47 46 45 44 43 
26 25 24 23 22 21 42 
27 10 9  8  7  20 41 
28 11 2  1  6  19 40 
29 12 3  4  5  18 39 
30 13 14 15 16 17 38 
31 32 33 34 35 36 37 
```

+ Size: 5
+ Clockwise

```
1  2  3  4  5  
16 17 18 19 6  
15 24 25 20 7  
14 23 22 21 8  
13 12 11 10 9  
```


+ Size: 3
+ Counterclockwise

```
1 8 7 
2 9 6 
3 4 5 
```
